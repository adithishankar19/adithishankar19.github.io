About Me

I am a third year PhD student at the Music Technology Group, Universitat Pompeu Fabra, Barcelona, supervised by Prof. Xavier Serra, Dr. Martín Rocamora and Dr. Gloria Haro.

My research focuses on Music Information Retrieval and Computer Vision, specifically multimodal music analysis and Music Source Separation. I work on real-world challenges in live music recordings, such as bleeding-aware source separation and gesture-guided systems.
