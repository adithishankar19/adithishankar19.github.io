About Me

I am a third-year PhD student at the Music Technology Group (MTG), Universitat Pompeu Fabra, Barcelona[cite: 5].

My research sits at the intersection of Music Information Retrieval (MIR) and Computer Vision, focusing on multimodal music analysis and music source separation[cite: 6]. I specialize in bleeding-aware separation for live recordings and gesture-guided systems[cite: 7].
